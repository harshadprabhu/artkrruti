The collection

कृति — made by hand, for the pooja ghar

ArtKrruti is a small stitching studio. Every thaal cover is pleated by hand, every toran is cut and finished individually, and the fabric is real paithani, khan and brocade — not printed imitation.

Tell us the colour of your mandir, the size of your thaali or door, and the festival you're preparing for. We'll match it. Orders for mandals, housing societies and wedding return gifts are taken in bulk with a discount.

Delivery across India · Made to order in 3–10 days
